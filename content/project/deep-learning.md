$\textbf{Definitions}$

Suppose $K$ is a totally real number field with the ring of integers $A$. Suppose $f(x)$ is a quadratic forms of signature $(n,1)$ defined over $K$ such that for every non-identity embedding $\sigma \colon K \to \mathbb{R}$ the form $f^{\sigma}$ is positive definite.
